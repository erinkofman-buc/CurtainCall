How It Works

Whether you're looking for the perfect costume or ready to pass one on, we've made it simple.

For Buyers

1

Browse & Discover

Search by category, size, or condition. Filter for ballet, jazz, skating, gymnastics, and more to find exactly what you need.

2

Ask Questions

Message the seller directly to ask about fit, fabric, or any details. Every listing includes measurements so you can be confident before you buy.

3

Buy Securely

Purchase through our secure checkout powered by Stripe. Your payment is held safely until the costume arrives.

4

Receive Your Costume

Your costume arrives at your door with tracking. Confirm it matches the listing and you're ready to take the stage.

For Sellers

1

Create Your Listing

Upload clear photos from multiple angles. Add accurate measurements, condition notes, and the costume's story if you'd like.

2

Set Your Price

You decide what your costume is worth. Check similar listings for guidance, or price based on condition and original value.

3

Connect Stripe

Link your Stripe account so you can receive payouts directly. Setup takes just a few minutes.

4

Ship When Sold

When a buyer purchases your costume, pack it carefully and ship within 5 business days. Add tracking and you're done.

Simple, Transparent Pricing

8%

platform fee per sale

That's it. No listing fees, no monthly subscriptions. Buyers pay the listed price plus shipping. Sellers keep 92% of the sale price.
