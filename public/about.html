About CurtainCall

Our Story

CurtainCall was born from the performing arts community. Every costume has a story — a first competition, a standing ovation, a final bow. We believe those stories deserve to continue.

Our marketplace connects Canadian dancers, skaters, and gymnasts so costumes find their next stage. Instead of sitting in a closet after a single season, a beautifully crafted costume can inspire another performer, light up another audience, and live on.

We built CurtainCall because we know how much goes into every performance — the training, the artistry, and yes, the costumes. They matter. And they deserve better than being forgotten.

What We Believe In

Transparency

Every listing includes detailed measurements and honest condition descriptions. You know exactly what you're getting before you buy.

Community

Built by performers, for performers. We understand the world of dance, skating, and gymnastics because we come from it.

Sustainability

Performance costumes are works of art. Extending their life keeps beautiful pieces out of landfills and on the stages where they belong.
